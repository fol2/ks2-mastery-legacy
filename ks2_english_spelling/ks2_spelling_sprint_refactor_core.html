KS2 Spelling Sprint

This app is built for fast, repeatable KS2 spelling practice. It makes children hear the word in a sentence, spell it from memory, fix mistakes straight away, and then see weak words again later. Progress is saved separately for each learner on this browser.

How this round works

Smart review

You hear a word, spell it from memory, and weak words come back later in the same session.

Play the word, listen to the whole sentence, then type the spelling. Press Enter to submit.
Wrong words are corrected immediately and repeated soon after. New words need more than one correct spelling in the same round before they are counted as secure for today.
